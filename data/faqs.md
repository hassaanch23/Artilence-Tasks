# Aurora Mobility — Customer Support FAQ

## What products does Aurora Mobility sell?
Aurora Mobility designs and sells electric commuter bicycles. Our flagship model is
the Aurora X1, released in September 2024.

## How much does the Aurora X1 cost?
The Aurora X1 is priced at $1,899 USD. Accessories such as spare batteries and
panniers are sold separately.

## What is the top speed and range of the Aurora X1?
The Aurora X1 has a top speed of 28 mph (45 km/h) and a range of up to 60 miles on a
single charge in Eco mode.

## How long does the battery take to charge?
A full charge takes about 4.5 hours from empty. A partial charge to 80 percent takes
roughly 3 hours. The 720 Wh battery is removable.

## What is the warranty policy?
Aurora Mobility provides a two-year warranty on the frame and a one-year warranty on
the battery and electronic components. The warranty does not cover accidental damage
or normal wear of tires and brake pads.

## What is your return policy?
Unused bikes may be returned within 30 days of delivery in their original packaging
for a full refund. Return shipping is free for customers in the United States.

## How long does shipping take?
Orders within the United States are delivered in 5 to 7 business days. Shipping is
free on orders over $50.

## Do you ship internationally?
Yes. We currently ship to Canada and the European Union. International orders take
10 to 14 business days and may be subject to import duties.

## How do I track my order?
A tracking link is emailed to you when your order ships. You can also view order
status any time in your account dashboard on our website.

## What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Klarna. Klarna offers four
interest-free installments.

## Is the Aurora X1 water resistant?
The Aurora X1 is rated IPX5, so it can handle rain and splashes, but it must not be
submerged in water or pressure-washed.

## How do I reset the bike if it will not turn on?
Hold the power button for 10 seconds to force a reset. If the display shows error
code E3, the motor is overheating; stop and let it cool for 15 minutes.

## Is there a mobile app?
Yes. The Aurora Ride app is available for iOS and Android. It pairs with the bike over
Bluetooth and provides GPS tracking, ride stats, and firmware updates.

## How do I contact a human support agent?
Email support@auroramobility.example. Our support team is available Monday to Friday,
9am to 5pm Pacific Time. Aurora Mobility is headquartered in San Diego, California.
